About Spigelmyer Plumbing, Heating & AC

Your Trusted Local Experts Since 1979

Spigelmyer Plumbing, Heating & Air Conditioning has been proudly serving the State College, PA area and surrounding communities for over four decades. Founded on principles of quality workmanship, reliability, and exceptional customer service, we have grown into a leading provider of residential and commercial plumbing and HVAC solutions.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the highest quality service and solutions for their plumbing, heating, and cooling needs. We strive to build lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and professionalism. Your comfort and safety are our top priorities.

Our Team

Our team consists of licensed, insured, and highly skilled technicians who are passionate about what they do. We invest in continuous training to stay updated on the latest industry advancements and technologies. When you call Spigelmyer, you can be confident that you're getting knowledgeable experts dedicated to solving your problems efficiently and effectively.

Our Commitment to the Community

As a locally owned and operated business, we are deeply committed to the State College community. We believe in supporting our neighbors and contributing to the place we call home. We value the trust our customers place in us and work hard every day to maintain that trust.
